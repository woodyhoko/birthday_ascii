A browser tool that maps any image's luminance to a density ramp of characters — plus a real-time 3D spinning cake drawn entirely in ASCII with a z-buffer and Lambertian shading.

Image → ASCII

Decodes onto a hidden canvas, samples each cell's perceptual luminance (BT.601 luma) and picks a character from a sparse-to-dense ramp. Export the result as a PNG.

Open the tool →
🍩

3D Spinning Cake

A torus parameterized by two angles, rotated each frame, projected with perspective, depth-sorted by a z-buffer and tinted with HSL for that rainbow-iced look.
